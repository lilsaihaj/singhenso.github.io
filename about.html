About the Artist

Gagandeep Singh known as “Singh” was born in Punjab, India in 1992 and has lived in Los Angeles his whole life. Singh is largely self-taught and is completing his Bachelor of Arts degree at Cal State University Northridge specializing in contemporary figurative painting. He appears to capture the very essence of his subjects quickly with skillful, loose brushwork... His work includes realistic portrait oil paintings with influences from the masters such as Velazquez, Rembrandt, Degas, Repin and Sargent. He is building up his portfolio for graduate school and this is an exciting time for the artist and for his collectors in what promises to be a brilliant future for this fresh California artist.
