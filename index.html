About the artist

Gagandeep Singh known as “Singh” was born in Punjab, India in 1992 and has lived in Los Angeles his whole life. Singh is largely self-taught and is completing his Bachelor of Arts degree at Cal State University Northridge specializing in contemporary figurative painting. He appears to capture the very essence of his subjects quickly with skillful, loose brushwork...
