About the artist

Gagandeep Singh was born in Punjab, India in 1992 and has lived in Los Angeles his whole life. Singh is largely self-taught and is completing his Bachelor of Arts degree at Cal State University Northridge specializing in contemporary figure and landscape painting. Singh's portraits- both in oil and watercolor- exemplify his creative talent in all media. His work includes realistic oil paintings with influences from artist such as Sargent, Zorn, Sorolla and Repin.
